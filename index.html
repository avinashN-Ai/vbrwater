Science of Purity

Our 5-Stage Purification
Process

Every drop of QUERA WATER undergoes a rigorous multi-stage purification journey, ensuring absolute safety and exceptional taste.

01
🪨
Sediment Filter

Removes dirt, dust, rust, and suspended particles larger than 5 microns from raw water.

02
🌊
Activated Carbon

Eliminates chlorine, chloramines, volatile organic compounds, and odour-causing chemicals.

03
💎
RO Membrane

Reverse osmosis removes 99.9% of dissolved solids, heavy metals, bacteria, and viruses.

04
✨
UV Sterilisation

Ultraviolet light destroys any remaining microorganisms without adding chemicals.

05
⚗️
Mineral Enrichment

Essential minerals are re-added in perfect balance for optimal taste and health benefits.
