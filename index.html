What We Do

Premium Water Sourcing & Bottling

We extract crystal-clear natural groundwater from deep underground aquifers, purify it with advanced technologies, enrich it with vital minerals, and bottle it for the ultimate hydration experience.

Sourcing ⛰️

Underground Sourcing

We extract pristine natural water from deep underground aquifers. Sourcing water from rich underground layers ensures natural pre-filtration and absolute isolation from surface pollutants.

  • Extracted from deep underground aquifers
  • Naturally rich in essential trace minerals
  • Untouched by surface chemical run-offs
  • Eco-friendly sourcing and sustainable extraction

Packaged Drinking Water

Science of Purity

5-Stage Purification Timeline

Every drop of QUERA WATER undergoes a rigorous multi-stage purification journey, ensuring absolute safety and exceptional taste.

🪨
Stage 01

Raw Water

Initial intake passing through multi-grade sediment filtration to clear suspended dust and rust particles.

🌊
Stage 02

RO Filtration

Reverse Osmosis removes 99.9% of excess dissolved solids, heavy metals, and micro-salts.

⚡
Stage 03

UV Treatment

High-intensity Ultraviolet sterilization blocks and destroys active organic microbes.

⚗️
Stage 04

Mineral Balance

Enriching filtered water with balanced traces of Calcium and Magnesium for a sweet, clean taste.

💧
Stage 05

Pure Drinking

Safe, refreshingly light, and FSSAI-certified premium drinking water bottled under sterile seals.
